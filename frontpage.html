FAQs

Common Questions

Do I need any technical knowledge to work with you? +
Absolutely not. We handle 100% of the technical side. You just need to tell us about your business and what you want — we take care of the rest from design to deployment.
How long does it take to build my website? +
You'll see your first design draft within 48 hours of consultation. The full website typically goes live within 5–10 business days, depending on the complexity of the project.
Do you offer websites in Hindi or regional languages? +
Yes! We can build multilingual websites that include Hindi, Marathi, or any other regional language your customers prefer to use.
What happens after the website is launched? +
We offer ongoing maintenance plans that cover security updates, content changes, performance monitoring, and monthly analytics reports. We're with you for the long term.
Will my website appear on Google? +
Yes. Every website we build includes basic SEO setup. Our Business and Pro plans include comprehensive local and on-page SEO to help you rank higher and get found by more customers.
Can I make changes to my website after launch? +
Yes. All sites come with a simple admin panel so you can update text, images, and basic content yourself. Or you can contact our support team and we'll handle it for you.
